conflict: same trigger, opposite actions
example:
-when I switch off the oven, switch off the light in the kitchen
-when I switch off the oven, switch on the light in the kitchen
solution: make opposite triggers OR change action OR change trigger completely

conflict: same trigger, overlapping conditions, opposite actions
example:
-when I enter the bedroom, if the light is on, turn off the TV
-when I enter the bedroom, if the light is on, turn on the television
solution: make opposite triggers OR change action OR completely change trigger OR change a condition so that they do not overlap

conflict: same trigger, 1 automation with condition and one not, overwrite actions
example:
-when I enter the kitchen, switch on the light
-when I enter the kitchen, switch on the light at 35% brightness
solution: add an OR condition exclude one of the two OR merge the two automations with different conditions

conflict: different triggers, opposite actions
example:
-when air quality is low, switch on the purifier
-when I leave the house, switch off the purifier
solution: it is a possible conflict, evaluate possible solutions

conflict:different triggers, subsumed actions
example:
-when I leave the house turn off all the lights
-when it is 5 o'clock turn on the living room light
solution: add condition OR exclude specific action device from general action devices
